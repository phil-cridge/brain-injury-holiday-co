Covid-19 Advice

The NHS Covid Pass lets you show others the details of your Covid-19 vaccine (or vaccines) when travelling abroad. In addition to your NHS Covid Pass, you will need to follow additional rules when travelling abroad.

This is the link to current government guidelines: www.gov.uk>guidance>tavel-abroad-from-england should you wish to have a look, but please be assured we will ensure everything is taken care of regarding Covid, before you travel.

But organising trips can be nearly impossible for people with a brain injury - that's why we specialise in exactly that. Utilise our experience and expertise to take care of everything and leave you with only one thing to do... pack your bags.

We'll even help you with that!

A simple process to take the hassle out of planning a holiday whilst still giving you all the information you need.

Break the routine and feel the lasting benefits of new experiences in stunning locations in the UK and around the world

From city breaks to mountain tops, adventures don't need to be a thing of the past

Give your carers and loved ones some respite. Leave them at home or bring them with you - you decide

Imagine looking forward to a holiday knowing an experienced and professional team is handling everything. Taking into consideration all of your care needs, we will design a bespoke package to provide you with a holiday of your choosing either in the UK or around the world.

Plan & book your holiday

Once we have a full understanding of your requirements from your free assessment we can discuss the options and put together a full proposal for your consideration.

Testing the water

We offer the option of a test trip for new clients who would like to experience the quality of our service and begin establishing a therapeutic relationship.

Embark on your stress free holiday
